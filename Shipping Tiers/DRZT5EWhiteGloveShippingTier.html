Oversized Item White Glove Delivery Service

  • Your oversized item in the shipping crate will be removed from the truck by the driver and placed on the ground by a hydraulic liftgate.
  • Someone must be available to accept delivery.
  • Delivery address must be accessible to a tractor-trailer. A driveway is considered “accessible” if it a) has no overhanging trees, b) has no overhanging power lines, c) contains no culverts or bridges, and d) will allow the truck to make a full turn-around. If the driveway isn’t accessible the delivery will be made at the base of the driveway.
  • Rush delivery is not available on oversized freight shipments.
  • After we process your order and it leaves our factory, the freight company will call you to schedule a delivery time. The carrier will usually provide a 3-4 hour delivery window (for example, 8am to Noon).
