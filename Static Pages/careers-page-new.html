WELLNESS AT DR

We operate in a fast-paced environment with unlimited opportunities for growth and advancement. Our facilities have the latest technology, our products lead the industry and our people blaze trails others only think about.

We apply our same philosophies on innovation and leadership to employee wellbeing. Our Healthy Living Program is an important element of our culture. The program provides opportunities for employees and their spouses to participate in activities that contribute to their overall health and wellness. Programs include ...

  • Benefit premium credits with completion of healthy living activities
  • Medical support programs: Maternity, Diabetes, Treatment Decision Support and more
  • Health savings accounts (with High Deductible Health Plan election)
  • Flexible spending accounts
  • Life and AD&D insurance
  • Short-term disability insurance
  • Long-term disability insurance
  • Voluntary income protection plans
  • 401(k) retirement savings plans with company match
  • Generac Stock ownership through the Employee Stock Purchase Plan
  • Work-life benefits: Paid vacation and holidays

DR® POWER EQUIPMENT. Professional Power Done Right.

We have been manufacturing and selling professional grade outdoor power equipment since 1985. From our earliest days, headquartered in a Vermont log cabin, we have been dedicated to creating equipment of enduring quality and uncompromising performance.
