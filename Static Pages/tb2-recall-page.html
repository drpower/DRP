TB2 Tow-Behind Field and Brush Mower Recall

The reason for the recall is the exhaust pipe can break and cause damage to the unit due to excessive heat, especially in the presence of dry cuttings or spilled fuel, posing fire and burn hazards.

DR Power is offering consumers the choice between a prorated refund or application of the prorated refund toward a new DR Power product. The prorated refund will be determined based on the age of the unit, in accordance with the below schedule:

Age of unit
 Refunded % of purchase price or value of credit
0 up to 5 years
100%
5 years up to 6 years
60%
6 years up to 8 years
30%


The recalled tow-behind field and brush mowers were sold from November 2015 through April 2021.

Ensure your TB2 is part of this recall.

The model and serial number are printed on a white label that is on the rear frame of the mower near the right rear tire.
