Shipping Overview

We work hard to get your order to you as quickly and efficiently as possible! Orders for in-stock items are shipped within one to two business days. Smaller items will be charged according to our Standard Shipping chart (below). Larger items will be charged according to either the Oversized Shipping chart (below) or the Motor Freight charges listed on each product's description page in the Shipping area. Most DR® Power Equipment machines ship via motor freight.

If your order contains both standard and oversized items, standard shipping charges will apply only to the standard sized items you order, and oversized shipping charges will apply only to the oversized items you order. Delivery estimates are for the contiguous 48 United States. Please contact us for rates to all other areas.

Rush Delivery

Expedited delivery is available for select items. Please see the Standard and Oversized shipping charts for shipping details and charges. Rush delivery is not available for items shipped via motor freight, which includes most DR® Power Equipment machines. Most expedited shipments arrive within 2 business days of when they ship from our warehouse (not from the date item is ordered). Items that do not leave our warehouse the same day they are ordered are noted in the checkout process.

Changes to Delivery After Shipment

Any request to change the delivery address or type of delivery service that is made after your product ships will be subject to a re-consignment fee. If you request a re-consignment, we will obtain a quote from the carrier and get your approval before we finalize the re-consignment.

Motor Freight Shipping

Most DR® Power Equipment machines are shipped via motor freight due to their size and weight. We offer four delivery methods for items shipped via motor freight. See the shipping charges listed in the shipping tab of each product's description page. If ordering online, you will be prompted to choose your prefered delivery option during checkout. Note that rush delivery is not available for items shipped via motor freight.

Depot Delivery

We ship to hundreds of trucking depots across the U.S. When we receive your order, we will determine the trucking depot closest to you. Most will load your machine into your vehicle (you'll need to have a pickup truck or trailer).

Business Delivery

Choose a business in your area to have your machine delivered to. The business you select must have a loading and off-loading facility and must accept delivery during normal business hours (without advance notification). Typical businesses used by our customers include feed stores, lumber yards, building supply centers, etc.

Home Delivery

Your home and driveway must be accessible to a tractor-trailer, and someone must be home to accept delivery. Because the driver is not required to unload your machine, you should plan on having a couple of sturdy 10-foot planks (2" thick x 10" wide, minimum) and at least one or two helpers to help remove the machine from the truck.

Enhanced Home Delivery navigatedown

Our most comprehensive option. Your machine will be removed from the truck by the driver and placed on the ground. Someone must be available to accept delivery, and your home and driveway must be accessible to a tractor-trailer.
