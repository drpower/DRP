Company

DR® POWER EQUIPMENT. Professional Power Done Right.

We have been manufacturing and selling professional grade outdoor power equipment since 1985. From our earliest days, headquartered in a Vermont log cabin, we have been dedicated to creating equipment of enduring quality and uncompromising performance. We believe that every minute spent working outdoors can be more productive, more enjoyable, and more satisfying when you have the right tools.

Today DR Power Equipment is a subsidiary of Generac Power Systems, Inc. and we offer an extensive line of gas- and battery-powered equipment that is available factory-direct or through our growing family of retail partners. Regardless of where you purchase DR Power Equipment, you can be assured of our commitment to service after the sale. Our call center is open seven days a week, and you can find parts and accessories on our website.

1985

DR POWER STARTED IN 1985

Originally incorporated as “Dick Raymond’s Country Home Products,” CHP is born. Fresh from Garden Way, Lyman Wood, Rich Alther, Dick Raymond, John Gibbons, and Bill Lockwood (joining in 1986) band together to launch their first product: The Dick Raymond Work/Rest Wagon.

1987

DR TRIMMER MOWER

The DR Trimmer Mower ushers in an all-new category in outdoor power equipment. The Revolutionary DR Trimmer on Wheels combines the best features of a handheld trimmer and a bladed mower into an easy-to-use lawn machine. Today, more than 800,000 DR Trimmers have been sold in the United States, making it America’s #1 selling trimmer on wheels.

1989

DR FIELD & BRUSH MOWER

The DR Field & Brush Mower provides an affordable alternative to a tractor-mounted brush mower. Over the years it evolves into a multi-attachment, all-season machine and the line expands to include tow-behind models. DR Field and Brush Mowers continue to set the industry standard for enduring quality and uncompromising performance.

1993

DR POWERAGON

The DR Powerwagon—a self-propelled, walk-behind hauler—is the company’s first venture into product manufacturing.

1993

DR OWNER NEWS

With a loyal owner base growing rapidly, DR launches the DR Owner News, a publication “By, For, and About DR Owners” which is chock full of stories about living in the country.

1996

DR ROTOTILLER

Aided by its founders’ experience with Troybilt® tillers, DR Power launches the DR Rototiller. Today, the DR Rototiller line includes small cultivators, walk-behind tillers, and tow-behind models for ATVs and tractors.

1996

DR LOG SPLITTERS

DR steps into the log splitter business, starting with a small electric benchtop model and, over time, designing a complete line of the world’s fastest kinetic splitters—DR RapidFire™ Log Splitters, and most powerful hydraulic splitters.

1999

DR CHIPPERS

Billed as the “World’s First Self-Feeding Chipper for Homeowners,” the DR Rapid Feed Chipper challenges the concept that only road crews can handle chipping large trees. With the Rapid Feed Chippers, DR introduces a lineup of dedicated chippers that can reduce tree limbs up to 6" in diameter to a pile of chips in just minutes.

2001

DR VERSA MOWER

As the popularity of the zero-turn lawnmower grows in the U.S. lawn and garden market, DR steps in with a Z-mower of its own—the DR Converta Mower (later renamed DR Versa Mower)—a zero turn mower which converts to a walk-behind mower with the flip of a lever.

2003

NEUTON LAWN MOWER

CHP goes green with the launch of Neuton Power Equipment, primarily featuring the Neuton Battery Lawn Mower. Within a few years, the Neuton Mower becomes the darling of California’s Clean Air lawn mower trade-in events, causing thousands of Californians to trade in their old gas machines for the new lightweight mower of the future.

2003

DR Leaf and Lawn Vacuum

DR takes on what is likely the most universally hated, weekend-killing chore of them all—fall leaf raking. The DR Leaf and Lawn Vacuum, designed to fit virtually any riding lawn mower, collects leaves while mowing, and reduces them to a fine, compost-ready mulch. With subsequent redesigns and the addition of walk-behind models, the Leaf and Lawn Vacuum becomes one of the company’s top products.

2006

DR Power Grader

As every rural homeowner knows, living with pot holes every spring is a car-battering battle. That is, until the introduction of the DR Power Grader. Towed behind a riding mower or ATV, the DR Power Grader allows homeowners to easily maintain their own driveways. It loosens and redistributes existing material, saving hundreds on the cost of commercial grading and adding new gravel.

2009

DR Towable Backhoe

The ultimate DIY excavating machine—the DR Towable Backhoe—gets digging done right at an affordable price. Using its own hydraulics and engine, the DR Backhoe is a go-anywhere excavator that doesn’t require investing in a tractor.

2010

Sunward Solar Hot Water

CHP leans into green energy with the introduction of Sunward Solar Hot Water Heaters: solar systems which use rooftop or ground-mounted solar collectors to gather heat from the sun and transmit it to hot water storage tanks inside the home.

2010

DR Dump Pro

The DR Dump Pro is designed to take the hard work out of unloading. Pickup trucks are standard equipment among DR homeowners.

2012

DR BURNCAGE

“Quick! Call 911!” is the headline that launches the DR BurnCage, an instant hit for all backyard burners. The launch is so successful that it was soon followed up with additional models: BurnCage XL and BurnCage MAX.

2014

DR TREE CHOPPER

For managing land areas prone to invasive “weed trees, especially in Texas, the DR Tree Chopper attaches to the front of an ATV, slicing through small field trees quickly. The product in action is so astounding that the Tree Chopper video quickly goes viral and the Tree Chopper web page becomes the number one visited page on the site.

2014

DR SP22 Lawn Mower

The first of the SP Mower series launches with the DR SP22. Billed as “the last mower you’ll ever own,” the SP22 is DR’s answer to box store throwaway mowers. In 2019 DR adds wide cut mowers to the line: SP26 and SP30.

2018

DR BATTERY POWERED TOOLS

DR Battery Powered Tools, employing an interchangeable 62V LiPRO™ battery, mark DR Power’s entrance into green power. In time for spring, the DR lineup includes 3 mowers (including a self-propelled model) and 4 handheld yard tools—all of them powered by the same quick-charging battery.

2018

DR SNOW BLOWERS

Ready in time for the first winter storms, DR Snow Blowers introduce feature-rich machines that include plug-in electric starting, EZ-Turn Steering™, and superior chute controls. Available in four widths, from 26" to 34" wide, the Snow Blowers boost company sales in what has traditionally been the “off-season”.

2019

DR PULSE TRIMMER MOWER

Encouraged by the early success of the Battery Powered Tools, DR engineers are able to leverage the same proven LiPRO™ battery platform to introduce the DR Pulse Trimmer/Mower, the world’s first battery powered trimmer on wheels.

2020

GENERAC ACQUIRES MEAN GREEN PRODUCTS

The company acquires Mean Green Products, entering into the market for electric powered Z-turn mowers.

2021

MEAN GREEN VANQUISH

Mean Green expands it's offering with a commercial, stand-on Z-turn mower capable of all day run times (up to 7 hours of continuous use). Available in 52" or 60" cutting width.

2021

DR XD SERIES FIELD & BRUSH MOWERS

The flagship DR Field & Brush Mower moves into the commercial and rental markets with the XD Series. The line includes 26- and 30-inch cuts, engines up to 18.5 HP, and 3-Year Commercial warranties.

2022

MEAN GREEN NEMESIS

The NEMESIS offers commercial electric peformance to users who don't require all day runtimes. This new rider will run for up to 2.25 hours — enough to mow up to 5 acres without recharging.

2022

DR ZT5E ELECTRIC Z-TURN MOWER

DR goes electric with its first zero-turn mower, an electric version that operates with one, two, or three unique battery packs. Allows consumers to mow up to 5 acres without recharging...and without gas!

2022

DR PILOT XT LEAF & LAWN VACUUM

DR expands its role as the Leader in Leaf Vacs with the introduction of the PILOT XT which fills standard paper leaf bags so they are ready for curbside pickup.

2023

MEAN GREEN FURY

Mean Green complements the VANQUISH (its full-sized stand-on mower) with the narrower FURY, specially designed to access gated areas that most commercial mowers are too wide for. Available in 32” or 36” cutting width.

2023

DR FB26E FIELD & BRUSH MOWER

DR becomes the first brand to produce a battery-electric mower for rough area mowing. The FB26E uses the same portable battery pack that powers the DR ZT5E Zero-Turn Mower.

2024

DR PRO MAX 60T FIELD & BRUSH MOWER

DR Power introduces its largest ever tow-behind field and brush mower – the PRO MAX60T features a 60” wide mowing deck powered by a 24HP Commercial Kawasaki OHV V-Twin engine.

2024

MEAN GREEN EVO 96

Mean Green introduces the EVO 96, the first wide-area electric zero-turn mower with a 96” mulching rear discharge deck that folds up to 72.5” for easy trailering and a 44kWh battery capable of running continuously for up to 8 hours.
