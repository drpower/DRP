DR® POWER EQUIPMENT. Professional Power Done Right.

We have been manufacturing and selling professional grade outdoor power equipment since 1985. From our earliest days, headquartered in a Vermont log cabin, we have been dedicated to creating equipment of enduring quality and uncompromising performance. We believe that every minute spent working outdoors can be more productive, more enjoyable, and more satisfying when you have the right tools.

Today DR Power Equipment is a subsidiary of Generac Power Systems, Inc. and we offer an extensive line of gas- and battery-powered equipment that is available factory-direct or through our growing family of retail partners. Regardless of where you purchase DR Power Equipment, you can be assured of our commitment to service after the sale. Our call center is open seven days a week, and you can find parts and accessories on our website.
